Montage Deer Valley

Montage Deer Valley is tailored to connoisseurs of all‐season adventure. With 220 rooms including more than 66 suites and residences, our mountain retreat grants true ski‐in/ski‐out access to one of America's best ski resorts. Guests enjoy refined accommodations, with gas fireplaces in every guestroom; a grand, alpine‐inspired Spa Montage; an après‐ski terrace with breathtaking mountain vistas; five inspired dining options, including Daly's Pub & Rec, complete with four regulation bowling lanes; and an on‐site ski/bike outfitter. Fly‐fishing, golf, horseback riding and other outdoor excursions are also close by. Minutes from Park City's Main Street, and just 36 miles from Salt Lake City International Airport, Montage Deer Valley welcomes you to a majestic playground where warm Western hospitality is second nature to every Montage associate.

Regardless of season, the tasteful venues of Montage Deer Valley offer a compelling array of customizable options for any occasion you bring to the mountains. Whether you're hosting a business meeting or a special event, our gracious service and innovative culinary offerings will ensure your gathering will not soon be forgotten. More than 60,000 square feet of indoor and outdoor event facilities create a customized canvas for everything from small gatherings to grand soirées. Elegant indoor settings include three stunning ballrooms, each with extensive pre‐function areas featuring floor to‐ceiling windows. Outdoor settings include a Grand Lawn ringed in mountain beauty and an alpine terrace overlooking the forested valley below. Supported by the latest business technology and gracious associates, your gathering will be perfectly customized and flawlessly executed.
