We're blessed to be in the business of making people happy. We believe in entertaining for a higher purpose, giving back, and paying it forward. So we decided to take it one step further and combine our mission and beliefs to offer our Signature and non-profit clients a way to participate with the introduction of Party Plus


Party Plus offers our Signature and non-profit clients the option to direct up to 10% percent of our management fee* on the planning of their event to a charity on our select list of participating organizations. If a charity of their choice is not already enrolled with Party Plus the client may choose to submit one for consideration and enrollment.

Lalela Project

Lalela Project provides educational arts to youth affected by extreme poverty sparking creative thinking and awakening the entrepreneurial spirit. Founded by Andrea Kerzner, Lalela addresses the physical needs of children affected by extreme poverty in a unique and creative way.The organization uses the power of the arts to connect with children and reach both their physical and mental self, empowering each child to see the possibilities in his or her future.
Ubuntu

Ubuntu is a Xhosa philosophy regarding human interconnectedness: I am because you are. The organization provides support to orphaned and vulnerable children in Port Elizabeth, South Africa — from healthcare to educational support — in order to achieve a simple yet radical mission: to help raise township children by providing them with what all children deserve — everything.

Phelophepa Train of Hope

The Phelophepa Train was established in 1994 as a way to leverage South Africa's rail infrastructure and bring crucial health care to communities that otherwise wouldn't have access to medical attention.

Infinite Family

Infinite Family was founded by Amy Stokes in 2003, after arriving in Johannesburg, South Africa with her husband to adopt their son. Infinite Family was created with the goal of nurturing, supporting and mentoring these children in an effort to get them off the street and help them become productive members of society.

Turning Point

Turning Point Foundation was established in 1988 and is governed by a board of directors drawn from the Ventura County community. Serving more than 500 clients each year through its shelter, supported housing and rehabilitation programs, in an effort to get them off the street and help them become productive members of society.

ONE

ONE is an international campaigning and advocacy organization of more than 6 million people taking action to end extreme poverty and preventable disease, particularly in Africa because the facts show extreme poverty has already been cut by 60% and can be virtually eliminated by 2030, but only if we act with urgency now.
